About

I am a researcher in the System Security and Privacy Group of Microsoft Research. Before joining Microsoft, I received my Ph.D. in Computer Science and Engineering from Penn State in 2016, and my B.Eng. in Software Engineering from Nanjing University in 2012. My research interests lie in the areas of systems and security.
